# Fruit-Quality-Classification

- An ML-Based Fruit Quality Classifier that:
  1. Counts the Total Number of Fruits (Apples for this project), and gives us the total number of fruits present in the image.
  2. Classifies Good, Fresh Fruits as Good and Bad, Rotten Fruits as Bad.
  3. Predicts the weight of fruits based on their size and shape.
  4. Based on the total weight of the fruit gives us a reasonable price based on the market price

- Made using:
  1. Python
  2. Pytorch / Tensorflow
  3. YOLOv8 (detection and counting)
  4. XGBoost (price predicition)
  5. Scikit-learn (ML utils)
